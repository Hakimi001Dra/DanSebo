About Us

Innovation, Excellence & Dedication

DanSebo Global Services Ltd is a Nigerian registered company (RC: 84133837) established with a commitment to providing cutting-edge IT solutions, digital services, professional printing and branding, entrepreneurial development training, and general consultancy services.

As a full-service printing and digital company, we bring creativity, innovation, and precision to every project. We cater to individuals, businesses, religious institutions, NGOs, schools, and government bodies — combining modern technology with skilled craftsmanship to deliver reliable, top-quality results.

We stand as a trusted partner to governments, businesses, and NGOs in achieving digital and operational excellence across Africa and beyond.

Our MissionEmpowering individuals and organizations with innovative IT solutions, premium branding, and marketing services fostering entrepreneurship and skill development.

Founder & CEO

Ahmad Ismail Waziri

Ismail Ahmad Waziri is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Dan Sebo Global Services. A passionate entrepreneur, digital skills advocate, and community-driven leader committed to creating impact through innovation, education, and quality service delivery.

A graduate of Kaduna State University (B.Sc Education in Biology), he has attained several professional and digital skill certifications. With a strong background in digital services, printing solutions, and capacity building, he established DanSebo with a clear vision: to build a trusted brand that empowers individuals, supports businesses, and creates sustainable opportunities for growth.
